In Fall 2018, I joined the UC Irvine Computer Science Department as a Associate Teaching Professor, SOE.

Prior to joing UCIrvine, I was a teaching faculty, an undergraduate academic advisor, and the 5-year BS/MS program advisor in the Computer Science Department at Stony Brook University. I mainly taught sophomore and junior level Computer Science systems and architecture courses. I received the CS Department Award for Undergraduate Education in 2012 and CS Department Award for Major Contributions to Undergraduate Education in 2016.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from UCLA. My research interests include wireless and distributed embedded systems, hardware intellectual property protection, and statistical optimization.

I enjoy traveling, baking, paper crafts, and family life with my husband and two boys.
